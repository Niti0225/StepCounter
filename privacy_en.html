Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 30, 2025

1. Data We Collect

We collect the following data you provide voluntarily: height, weight, and gender. This data is used only for step tracking calculations and remains stored locally on your device. It is never transmitted to any external server.

2. Sensor Data & Device Access

The app uses your phone's motion sensors (accelerometer and step counter) to track physical activity. This data is processed locally and not shared externally.

3. Usage Data and Analytics

We may collect anonymous technical data such as step counts, usage patterns, and crash logs. This helps improve performance and user experience.

4. Advertising & Tracking

We use Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect device ID, IP address, and interaction data. This may be used for personalized advertising. Learn more: Google Ads Policy

5. Subscriptions

Subscriptions remove ads and are handled securely by your app store. Monthly and yearly options are available.

6. Push Notifications

The app may send notifications (e.g. reminders or updates). You can disable them in your device settings.

7. External Links

The app links to external platforms (Instagram, Snapchat, Twitter). These sites may collect data under their own policies.

8. Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from them. Contact us if you believe this has occurred.

9. Your Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your data, or withdraw consent. Contact: stay_active@outlook.de
